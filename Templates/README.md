# Recruitment Templates

This folder contains standardized recruitment templates designed to support key stages of the end-to-end hiring lifecycle. These templates demonstrate structured documentation practices commonly used by Talent Acquisition teams to improve consistency, collaboration, and hiring efficiency.

## Files Included

### Hiring_Manager_Intake_Template.xlsx
A structured template used during recruiter and hiring manager discussions to capture hiring requirements before initiating the recruitment process.

**Key Sections**
- Requisition details
- Hiring priorities
- Required and preferred skills
- Experience requirements
- Compensation range
- Work location and work model
- Interview process
- Target hiring timeline

---

### Candidate_Screening_Template.xlsx
A standardized screening template for evaluating candidates during the initial recruiter screening stage.

**Key Sections**
- Candidate information
- Experience and compensation
- Notice period
- Skills assessment
- Communication evaluation
- Technical assessment
- Culture fit
- Overall recommendation
- Recruiter notes

---

### Interview_Feedback_Template.xlsx
A structured feedback form used by interviewers to capture evaluation outcomes and support objective hiring decisions.

**Key Sections**
- Interview details
- Technical competency
- Problem-solving ability
- Communication skills
- Culture fit
- Overall rating
- Hiring recommendation
- Strengths
- Areas of concern
- Additional comments

---

## Purpose

These templates promote:
- Standardized hiring documentation
- Consistent candidate evaluation
- Effective recruiter and hiring manager collaboration
- Structured interview feedback
- Transparent and data-driven hiring decisions

> **Note:** These templates were created for portfolio demonstration purposes and reflect industry-standard recruitment documentation practices.
